Ingridents:
    1. One cup of rice
    2. Two eggs
    3. Mixed vegetable

Instructions:
    1. Cook the rice and leave it for a few hours until dry
    2. Crach and breat the eggs (mix the yoke and white)
    3. Heat up the pan and add 1 tbs of oil
    4. Add the rice to the pan and break up big chuncks
    5. Add the egg and quickly mix and stir
    6. Add the vegetables
